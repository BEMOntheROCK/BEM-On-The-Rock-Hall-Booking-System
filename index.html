Process

How It Works

🏛️

1. Choose a Hall

Browse our spaces and select the one that fits your event.

📝

2. Submit a Request

Fill in your event details and submit the booking request form.

📞

3. We'll Contact You

Our team will reach out to confirm availability and discuss details.

✅

4. Pay & Confirm

Pay the deposit to secure your booking. Balance is due 3 weeks before your event.

Rules & Reminders

Before You Book

  • ①

    Confirmation & Deposit must be completed 3 weeks before the event date.

  • ②

    Rehearsal & Decoration time is provided the day before the event (5pm – 9pm).

  • ③

    Hospitality & Catering is fully the responsibility of the renter.

  • ④

    Cleanliness is the responsibility of the renter after the event.
